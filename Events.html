ARCHERS FROM OTHER CLUBS
Archers from other Clubs are always most welcome to shoot with us as our Guests, whether Indoors at Gweek Village Hall or Outside at our Field near Garras.
Please contact a Club Officer or any other Committee Member to make arrangements.
A valid Archery GB card will be required.
TASTER SESSIONS
We hold taster sessions at the club by prior mutual
arrangement with your group - Youth Club, Scouts, WI, Anyone!
These sessions offer complete novices the opportunity to have an Archery ‘Taster’
Two hours under the supervision of an Experienced Archer – perhaps use it as an experiment to see if you would like to do the Archery &
Club Induction Course, or maybe just as something a bit different for you and your friends to enjoy.
