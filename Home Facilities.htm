We are lucky enough to own a private field at Shee Glen, nr Garras,TR12 6LN.

There is a purpose built clubhouse with running water and toilet facilities, which gives our members exclusive access to shooting facilities 365 days of the year.

POSTCODE - TR12 6UG

To find the village hall in Gweek follow the signs to the National Seal Sanctuary and the hall car park is just to the left. There is plenty of parking available.

Gweek Village Hall, 24 Cryben, Gweek, Helston, TR12 6UG

POSTCODE - TR12 6LN

From Helston take A3083 towards The Lizard. Go past RNAS Culdrose and turn left at the next roundabout onto the B3293 towards St Keverne. Bear right at the next roundabout continuing towards St Keverne. Go through Garras village and the club ground is on the right as you leave the village, the entrance to the field has a concrete apron.
